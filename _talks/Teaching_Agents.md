[From Chatbots to Agents](https://youtu.be/ZdxtN3y6Wpk) traces the evolution from simple chatbots to powerful AI agents, demonstrating how tools like Claude Code enable agentic workflows. I'll walk you through how AI agents can actively plan, execute, and iterate on complex tasks—going far beyond simple question-and-answer interactions. Using real-world examples from sport management research, we explore agent skill development and multi-agent system design that can transform your research and content creation workflows. For additional resources and examples, check out the [AI-Conversation](https://github.com/TyrealQ/AI-Conversation) repository.
